Basket

Introduction

Basketball is a team ball sport that is played exclusively by hand.
Two teams of 5 players play against each other during 4 quarters, each of which lasts 12 minutes (the length of the quarters may vary according to the championship).
The goal of this sport is to make the most baskets and therefore score the most points.
When you score a basket, it is worth 2 or 3 points depending on where the player is on the court when he shoots.
Players can also do "dunks" which consist of dropping the ball in the hoop with one or two hands while holding on to it in a spectacular way.
